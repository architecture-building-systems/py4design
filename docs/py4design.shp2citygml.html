Pypolygon_list is a 2d list of tuples. A pypt is a tuple that documents the xyz coordinates of a pt e.g. (x,y,z). A pypolygon or pyptlist is a list of pypt e.g. [(x1,y1,z1), (x2,y2,z2), …].
